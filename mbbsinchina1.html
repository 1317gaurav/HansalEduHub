Submission of Additional Documents


All applicants must submit the academic documents. It includes

  • Mark sheets of class 10
  • Mark sheets of class 12
  • School transfer certificate
  • Conduct certificate and
  • School leaving certificate.

In addition, the other necessary documents like passport copies, application forms, etc.
In addition, there are few additional documents that applicant should send to process the admission. It includes, NO Criminal Record certificate and Medical Checkup Certificates. It is mandated by China MBBS Education Institution.
These two documents were mandatory for Indian students to submit when they apply for 30 day student visa in the Chinese Embassy. However, these documents are mandatory now to be attached along with the application forms when you apply for to study MBBS in China, to any Medical University in China.

MBBS in China for Indian Students 2019

In most cases, the academic year begin from the month of September or October. The application shall be closed by the end of June every year. It will take three to five weeks to process the documents. So, when you apply study in China immediately after the announcement of admission, you will have sufficient time to stay prepared.
Get the list of university you wish to join and mark the timeline for admission. Each university enroll different number of students every year.
MBBS is China is like to come with the same scenario. The rules and regulations as well as the admission process, also remains the same.

MBBS in China Duration

MBBS in China is a six years course, divided into two parts, classroom education and internship training for one year.
Indian students who study MBBS in China, has more advantages over other international students. Indian students do internship for one year after completion of the 5 year medical degree. After completion of the 5 years course and one year internship, there is no need for Indian students to do internship in India. Internship in China is valid in India, while internship in other countries doesn’t have validity in India. A few approved universities doesn’t need the students to undergo one year internship training. Students also have the option to skip internship in China and do the same in India. MCI screening test is necessary.


MBBS in China for Indian Students Eligibility, Fee and Lifestyle

Here is a summary of MBBS in China reviews, meant for Indian students.

Eligibility and admission is easy

For Indian students eligibility is simple, where most of the students who apply for the course gets admission.

Number of MCI approved universities

As stated above, the Medical Council of India approves many universities to study MBBS in China for Indian students. It gives more choice to choose the university according to the location, budget and other requirements.
China, being a vast country has different climate conditions. Students can find the university according to the climate of the specific region as they fit in.

Lifestyle

Indian students can easily adapt to the culture of the country. China is one of the countries that carry the rich tradition, heritage and ancient practices. It is easy for an Indian student to set bind into Chinese lifestyle and practices.

Multicultural environment for MBBS students in China

Students live in a multicultural environment, which makes living easy in a foreign land. Highly civilized country doesn’t make a mess for the foreign students to make a living.

Employment advantage

China has the fastest growing economy in the whole world. So, it is easy for the students to get employed in China itself. It is an additional career option available for Indian students to pursue a career in medical field in China itself.

Safety and security

China makes one of the safest countries for the Indian students and provides utmost security.

Language

Language is not a barrier, although mandarin is the major language of communication. All international students who study MBBS in China should study Chinese during the first year. It serves dual purpose.

  • Students can easily communicate with the patients during the training
  • It makes communication outside the campus and hostel easy. Students can really enjoy and explore the culture.
Food

Not to miss the interesting flavors of unique Chinese cuisine. Food cost is very low in china, for both Chinese and international cuisine. College and university campus provides low cost, quality food to the students. For average mid-range restaurants and hotels, it costs from 4 to 8 USD.

Scholarship

Chinese government provides the scholarships to the International students. Indian students can get the scholarships based on academic performance. Some of the scholarships include:

  • CGS: Chinese Government Scholarship;
  • US: University Scholarships;
  • CIS: Confucius Institute Scholarship;
  • CLGS: Chinese Local Government Scholarship
Cost of education and living

MBBS in China fees is low structure, easily affordable. Not only MBBS in China for Indian students is affordable but also the lifestyle. Cost of living in China is very reasonable. When compared with most of the European countries, China is the less expensive. Food, travel and living cost is easily affordable for a decent living.

MBBS in China FAQs

Q1. What is the medium teaching in medical education in China?

Most of the universities have English medium of teaching which is great for international students.

Q2. How long it takes to get student visa from China?

It does not take much time to obtain student visa in China, generally it takes 4-7 days and maximum 15 days.

Q3. Is MBBS degree from China valid in India?

MCI has approved more than 45 universities to study medicine. MBBS degree from these chines medical universities is valid in India after passing MCI-screening test.

Q4. What is the duration of MBBS program in China?

There are 5 & 6 years medicine program but majority of the universities offers 5 years degree.

Q5. What is the scope after MBBS in China?

The medicine degree from china has worldwide recognition. After studying medicine from China, students have options. He/she may practice in India after MCI-screening or do PG from abroad like Germany and USA.
