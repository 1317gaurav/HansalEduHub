Eligibility for MBBS in Bangladesh

  • He/She must have completed the age of 17 years on or before 31st December of the admission to the MBBS course.
  • There should not be more than one year gap. If a student wishes to apply then he/she must be a class XIIth pass out from the 2019 year batch or from 2020 current batch.
  • Students having a passing certificate of 2019 and 2020 shall only apply.
  • All Non-Bangladeshi students who are from India, Maldives, Sri Lanka or any other gulf countries who have passed 10th /12th or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and biology.
