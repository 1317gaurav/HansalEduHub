Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy: What is it About?

Ayurveda is the field of medicine which is growing in prominence from past few years. Ayurveda as an emerging field lays out a plethora of opportunity for young aspirants who wish to pursue their career in the field of Ayurveda. Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy lays across a platform for candidates to learn in depth about the different medicines and techniques used to cure ailments. The program is an integrated study of Ayurveda as a field combining it with the pharmacy.
Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy course enables candidates to know about the chemical properties of ayurvedic medicines, the proportion, composition and the amount in which it should be taken by patients. They are instilled with the fundamental principles of Ayurveda and its uses in treating different ailments. It builds a strong base for those who wish to pursue a higher qualification in the field such as Bachelors and Masters. As this course can be pursued by candidates after class 12th, the program acts as a foundation for growing their knowledge about the subject.
Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy curriculum is divided into theory and practice. The course primarily deals in drugs and its medications used in Ayurveda as a field of medicine. The topics focus on manufacturing and consumer sector of drugs including subjects such as ayurvedic formulations, Ayurveda drug manufacturing, quality control, drug store management, drug dispensing, modern and traditional manufacturing techniques as well as Ayurveda raw materials. Apart from this, they are also rendered additional knowledge through seminars, discussions, case studies, and presentations.
Aspirants of Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy have wide opportunities in career front in different areas of Ayurveda. They have opportunities in the areas of Ayurveda Hospitals both government and private sector, Medical Shops, Pharmaceutical Companies, Colleges, Universities and much more. The course aims to build the candidates with the knowledge of the sector by making them expertise in Ayurvedic medicine and for taking up higher educational programs.

Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy: Admission Process

Candidates seeking admission to Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy are required to meet the eligibility criteria as prescribed by respective colleges. The minimum eligibility criterion for this program is passing 10+2 with a minimum of 50% aggregate in any relevant stream from a recognized board. Most of the colleges carry on the admission procedure based on Merit obtained in the qualifying examination. However, certain colleges may conduct their own test for selecting candidates for the program. However, the eligibility criteria is subject to vary for different colleges and institutions.
Admissions to the Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy course is solely done on the basis of merit obtained in the qualifying examination. Candidates can apply for the course both online as well as offline. All important information pertaining to admission dates and selection of the candidate will be notified through email or listed on the official website of the college.


Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy: Syllabus and Course Description

Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy is set with a view of imparting candidates the knowledge of Ayurveda and the medications involved in its study. The course enables candidates to gain an intrinsic view of Ayurveda and the various methodology of treating various diseases. The methodology makes it easier for candidates to gain a better understanding of the subject thereby building a stronger base for students who wish to make their career in the field of Ayurveda as well as take up higher qualification such as Bachelors and Masters in Ayurveda.
The curriculum is divided into classroom theory and practice. Students are made proficient in overall all aspects of becoming professional Ayurveda specialists. The topics covered in Diploma in Ayurvedic Pharmacy course include Introduction to Ayurveda, Pharmaceutical Biology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Rasasastra and much more. The course also includes seminars and presentations from experts of the field.
